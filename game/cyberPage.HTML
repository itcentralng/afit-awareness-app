Slide 1: Strong Passwords:

Use complex combinations of characters
and avoid easily guessable information to
secure accounts.

Slide 2: Phishing Awareness:

Avoid clicking on suspicious links
or downloading attachments from
unknown sources in emails.

Slide 3: Software Updates:

Regularly update software to patch security
vulnerabilities and prevent cyber threats.

Slide 4: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):

Add an extra layer of security by enabling
two-factor authentication wherever possible.

Slide 5: Data Backup:

Ensure regular backups of important files to
prevent data loss due to
cyberattacks or hardware failures.
